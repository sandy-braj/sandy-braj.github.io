Pearson's analysis found that roles facing fast automation included receptionists, waiters, bank workers, retail sales assistants, translators, accountants, and bookkeepers, with many affected occupations female-dominated.

Sandya Baratha Raj, Director of Data Science at Pearson, told ABC News: "Some of the key jobs that are most impacted by technology are things like receptionists, waiters, bank workers, retail sales assistants."

She added: "Females are more likely to be negatively impacted by technology than males."

On workforce response, she said: "It's about being able to be proactive and understanding how we can use our data to make sure that people have the right skills for the future."
