Daily Telegraph article published on 6 June 2025.

The piece examined growing anxiety about AI replacing workers as language models become increasingly persuasive and human-like in how they communicate.

Sandya Baratha Raj, Director of AI and Analytics at Faethm by Pearson, said that the core limitation of large language models is contextual understanding.

She noted: "What they lack is contextual knowledge, knowledge about you, your role, your organisation and your clients."

She added that this context can be the difference between a strong decision and a poor one, reinforcing the importance of human judgment when using AI outputs in real work.
