ServiceNow and Pearson modelled the impact of 16 technologies on more than 6,000 roles in Australia and five other markets, decomposing roles into 26,000 tasks to estimate automation and augmentation effects.

The release cites Pearson Workforce Skills Solutions Director of Data Science Sandya Raj on the worker impact: "It's not all doom and gloom for workers in retail and banking. Our data reveals that technology can help remove the tedious and repetitive tasks, letting workers focus on higher-value, strategic activities."

She added: "To stay ahead in this tech-driven landscape, companies and individuals need to focus on skills that are less susceptible to automation and learn to collaborate with AI rather than resist it."
