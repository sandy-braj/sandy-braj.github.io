Understanding the skills and proficiency levels required for different roles is crucial for workforce planning, learning, and development. This paper proposes a skill proficiency modeling framework that helps describe, assess, and develop proficiency in key skills.

The framework includes a skill proficiency description pipeline that generates statements describing requirements at each proficiency level of a skill, followed by a skill proficiency by occupation model built from large-scale job ad data.

The paper also presents a visual analytics system based on a real-world career pathway scenario to demonstrate practical usefulness and effectiveness.
