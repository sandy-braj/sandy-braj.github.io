The widespread adoption of advanced technologies, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, and robotics, is transforming work tasks and required skill sets. This paper proposes a parametric mathematical model for predicting adoption rates of emerging technologies through a data-driven pipeline.

The approach uses global indicators for countries to predict technology adoption curves across countries and industries. It is intended to support policymakers and business leaders with scenario planning, learning and development strategy, and technology investment decisions.
